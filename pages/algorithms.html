What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is a list of steps used to do something.

A simple idea

Algorithms are everywhere. When you follow steps to tie your shoes, brush your teeth, or make a sandwhich, you are following an algorithm.

Sandwhich Algorithm

  1. Get two slices of bread.
  2. Put cheese on one slice.
  3. Close the sandwich.
  4. Eat and enjoy.

Why do computers need algorithms

Computers need clear steps because they do not guess. They only follow the instructions we give them.
