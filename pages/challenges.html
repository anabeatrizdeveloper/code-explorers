Little Challenges

Let’s test your explorer brain with simple logic activities.

Challenge 1

Which comes first in this algorithm?

  • Put on your shoes
  • Put on your socks

Answer: Put on your socks first.

Challenge 2

A robot gets this instruction:

Jump 3 times.

What will the robot do?

Answer: It will jump, jump, and jump again.

Challenge 3

If a character touches fire, it loses 1 life.

What happens if it touches fire?

Answer: It loses 1 life.
