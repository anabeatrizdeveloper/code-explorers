What Are Input and Output?

Computers receive information and show results.

What is input?

Input is information that goes into a computer, like clicking a button or typing a name.

What is output?

Output is what the computer shows back, like text on the screen, a sound, or an image.

Explorer Fact

When you press a key on the keyboard, that is input. When you see letters on the screen, that is output.
