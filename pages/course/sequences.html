What Is a Sequence?

A sequence is the correct order of steps.

Order matters

In coding, steps need to happen in the right order. That order is called a sequence.

Example

To brush your teeth, you first get your toothbrush, then add toothpaste, and then brush.

Explorer Fact

If the steps are in the wrong order, the computer may do the wrong thing.
