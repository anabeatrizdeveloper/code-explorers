What Is a Variable?

A variable is like a box where a computer keeps information.

Think of a box

Imagine you have a box with a label on it. The label says score. Inside the box, you can place a number.

In coding, that labeled box is called a variable.

Example

score = 10

This means the variable called score is storing the number 10.

Why is this useful?

Variables help computers remember things like names, points, colors, and lives in a game.

Explorer Fact

In games, variables can store coins, health, and time left to finish a mission.
