What Is a Loop?

A loop tells a computer to do something again and again.

Repeat, repeat, repeat

Sometimes computers need to repeat the same action many times. A loop helps them do that without writing the same instruction again and again.

Example

Jump 3 times

A computer can use a loop to make a character jump three times.

Instead of writing jump, jump, jump, we can say: repeat 3 times.

Where do we see loops?

Loops are used in games, animations, and apps whenever something needs to happen over and over.

Explorer Fact

In games, loops can make stars sparkle, enemies move, or music keep playing.
